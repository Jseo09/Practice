The Best Movie According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all time.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a science fiction film that explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the human spirit. It is a visually stunning and emotionally powerful film that will leave you in awe.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a superhero film that follows the story of Batman as he faces off against the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to bring chaos to Gotham City. It is a thrilling and intense film that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a drama film that follows the story of a man named Andy Dufresne is sentenced to life in prison for a crime he did not commit. It is a powerful and moving film that explores themes of hope, redemption, and the human spirit.
